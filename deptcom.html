DEPARTMENT COMMITTEE

Committee Overview

Purpose: The Academic Curriculum Committee is responsible for overseeing the development, evaluation, and continuous improvement of the academic curriculum. The committee ensures that the curriculum aligns with the institution's educational mission, meets accreditation standards, and prepares students for success in their academic and professional careers.

Scope: The committee reviews and approves course proposals, ensures curriculum alignment with academic standards, and monitors program effectiveness through assessments.

Goals: Enhance curriculum quality, ensure accreditation compliance, and promote innovative teaching practices to improve student outcomes.
